Frequently Asked Questions

Galaxy S25 Ultra comes in four stunning colors: Titanium Black, Titanium Gray, Titanium Violet, and Titanium Yellow. Each color features a premium titanium frame with a sophisticated matte finish.

Galaxy S25 Ultra features a refined design with a titanium frame that's both lightweight and ultra-durable. The device has slightly curved edges for improved ergonomics and comfort, while maintaining the iconic squared-off design. It has the toughest glass ever used on a Galaxy device and a flatter display for improved S Pen functionality.

Galaxy S25 Ultra brings several improvements over the S24 Ultra, including the Snapdragon 8 Elite processor for enhanced performance and efficiency, advanced Galaxy AI features, improved camera capabilities with the new ProVisual Engine, and refined design with better ergonomics. The S25 Ultra also offers enhanced battery life and faster charging capabilities.

Galaxy S25 Ultra includes a comprehensive suite of Galaxy AI features such as Circle to Search with Google, Live Translate for real-time conversation translation, Note Assist for summarizing and formatting notes, Chat Assist for communication help, Transcript Assist for recording and transcribing meetings, and advanced photo editing capabilities like Generative Edit and Portrait Studio.

The Galaxy S25 Ultra camera features the new ProVisual Engine, which enhances image quality in all lighting conditions. The 200MP main camera has improved low-light performance, and the AI-powered Object-Aware Engine ensures accurate subject recognition and natural skin tones. Video recording has been enhanced with better stabilization and 4K HDR capabilities, while the Portrait mode delivers studio-quality results.

Galaxy S25 Ultra features a robust 5000mAh battery that provides all-day usage. With the efficient Snapdragon 8 Elite processor, you can get up to 31 hours of video playback. The device supports 45W fast charging that can deliver hours of power in just minutes, as well as wireless charging and Wireless PowerShare for charging other devices.

Galaxy S25 Ultra features a titanium frame that provides enhanced durability while remaining lightweight. It's protected by the toughest Gorilla Glass ever used on a Galaxy device, offering superior scratch and drop protection. The device is IP68 rated for water and dust resistance, allowing submersion in up to 1.5 meters of freshwater for up to 30 minutes.

Galaxy S25 Ultra delivers exceptional gaming performance with the Snapdragon 8 Elite processor and 12GB of RAM. The vapor chamber cooling system prevents overheating during extended gaming sessions. The 6.8-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X display with 120Hz refresh rate provides smooth visuals, while the enhanced Ray Tracing capabilities deliver console-quality graphics on mobile.

Yes, Galaxy S25 Ultra comes with the S Pen built into the device. The S Pen has been improved with lower latency for a more natural writing experience. It supports all the familiar S Pen features like Air Commands, Screen Off Memo, Samsung Notes, and now includes enhanced AI capabilities such as Note Assist for summarizing and formatting your notes.

Yes, you can easily transfer your data from iOS using Samsung Smart Switch. This tool allows you to transfer contacts, photos, messages, notes, calendar events, and even some apps from your iPhone to your new Galaxy S25 Ultra. The process can be done wirelessly or using a USB cable for faster transfer of large data amounts.

Galaxy S25 Ultra features Samsung Knox security platform that protects your data with multiple layers of defense. Personal Data Engine processes sensitive information on-device, while Knox Vault secures biometric data in a physically isolated environment. The device includes features like Secure Folder for private files, Auto Blocker to prevent unauthorized installations, and Privacy Dashboard to monitor app permissions.
